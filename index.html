BomberFriends

by Lu Yang

How to Play

**press 'T' to toggle music and 'Y' to toggle sound effects**


You control a character that can drop bombs. The goal is to blow up the other player!

Characters

Your character starts out with 3 lives and you can only drop 1 bomb at a time. Your bomb is very weak, so pick up powerups to increase the number of bombs you can drop and your bombs' strength.

Player 1 Controls

To Move: W, A, S, D
To Drop Bomb: V

Player 2 Controls

To Move: I, J, K, L
To Drop Bomb: P

Powerups

A Flame Powerup increases the blast length of your bombs by 1

A Bomb Powerup increases the number of bombs you can drop at a time by 1

A Speed Powerup increases your speed by a little bit
