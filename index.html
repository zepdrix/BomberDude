**press 't' to toggle music**

**press 'y' to toggle sound effects**

How to Play

You control a character that can drop bombs. The goal is to blow up the other player!

Powerups

You can only drop 1 bomb at a time when you first spawn. A bomb powerup adds 1 to how many you can drop at a time!

The bombs you start out with are very weak, but flame powerups will increase their strength!

Movement speed can also be increased with the speed powerup!

Player 1 Controls

To Move: W, A, S, D
To Drop Bomb: V

Player 2 Controls

To Move: I, J, K, L
To Drop Bomb: P
